The Project

Our project is meant to cause a significant shrinkage on man. This is being done on light of the current climate crisis, as we believe we can put a stop to this by reducing our carbon footprint, we want to take that first step

Facts

  1. Did you know a 20% increase in height results in a 75% increase in weight?
  2. Old standards for the measurement of people allowed into size-limited boats are obsolete now, and they have resulted in 25 capsizings. This is because of the overall growth of the size of the average American

The Science

In 2000, adult vertebrates pertaining to the Marine Iguana species were found to be able to shrink themselves to 20% of their size several times during their lifetime. We are using that fact in order to investigate more on how this process works and how to use it for our own good and for the planet's.

Expectations

We expect that at the end of this project, in around a decade, we will have made a reduction of human body size down to 20% its original size, that means, a 170cm tall person would measure now 139cm. That is still 9 cm taller than our objective, but if we make the shrinkage proportional, we could achieve a 73% weight reduction, that is a lot of energy saved!
