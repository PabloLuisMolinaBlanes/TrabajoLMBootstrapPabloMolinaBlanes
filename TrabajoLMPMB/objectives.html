Objectives


We are trying to achieve the following objectives:

Human race to 130cm

Less size, less energy, we chose this height as it is 9 centimeters shorter than all known pygmy tribes, and also the average height of a current 7-8 year old, this could allow us to occupy less space (remember when you used to hide in now too small places? We do), shorten our infrastructure, require less feeding and help our planet.

Reusage of our current infrastructure

Recreate it to adapt it to our size and make the transition as seamless as possible.

Free up the knowledge

We believe this is too important for everyone to have just a few involved, the follow-ups of this project will be uploaded on WikiUniversity, a totally free knowledge repository where original research (our type) is welcome to be modified on and tracked fully.

End emissions

Such a significant reduction of size could make some really wasteful forms of energy too powerful for our needs, at our current size, we have ended with the coal mining industry in Spain, the last one closing in 2019, imagine how much now wasteful factories would become unnecesary, and thus, bring the emission of greenhouse gases to a non-existent state.

End the climate crisis

If our energy saving objectives are reached (0% of greenhouse gas emissions), due to them being too powerful for our now small bodies, we could start a long process of transition taken appropiate measures are taken to prevent possible regrowth, which may lead the planet to carbon levels before the Industrial Revolution!
